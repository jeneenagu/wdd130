Experience the adrenaline rush of whitewater rafting with Evercurrent Adventures. From thrilling rapids, spectacular views, to unforgettable outdoor getaways. Our carefully planned excursions ensure a safe, fun-filled adventure for everyone.

Contact us for more information!

Half-Day Rapids Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this adventure offers and exciting introduction to whitewater rafting. Participants will paddle through Class I and Class II rapids while enjoying scenic views of forests, rock formations, and local wildlife. Our experienced guides provide safety instruction and paddling techniques before the trip begins. The four-hour adventure includes all necessary rafting equipment and transportation to and from the launch site.

Minimum age: 10 years Price: $60 per person

Emerald River Cruise

A slow-moving river winds through lush green cliffs, dense canopies and hidden waterfalls. Small wooden boats glide quietly across clear waters, revealing fish darting beneath the surface. Stops along the riverbank allow short nature walks where flowers and wildlife abound. Ideal for photography and quiet reflection.

Price: $45 per person

Lakeside Wildridge Camp

Located on a raised ridge overlooking a waterfall pouring into a large, calm lake, this campsite blends water and mountain scenery. Mornings begin with mist rising off the lake while evenings bring stunning reflections of the sunset across the water. Activities include hiking, fishing, kayaking. Ideal for groups looking for adventure and relaxation.

Price: $400 per person
