Cross River

Cross River State is a coastal state in South-South Nigeria and famously known as the "People's Paradise". The state borders Cameroon to the east, Benue to the north, and Akwa Ibom/Abia to the west with Calabar as its capital city. Key features include tourism (Obudu Mountain Resort), rich biodiversity (Cross River National Park), and the annual Calabar Carnival.
